1. **Overnight Oats**: Start your morning with a bowl of overnight oats made from organic oats. Just mix rolled oats with almond milk, and let it sit overnight. In the morning, add sliced green bananas, a sprinkle of flaxseeds for those omega-3s, and a drizzle of raw honey to sweeten it up. This combo offers a good dose of fiber, healthy fats, and satisfying sweetness that keeps you full longer!
2. **Fruit Salad**: Pair your oats with a colorful fruit salad! Chop up some fresh berries, bananas, and a handful of nuts or seeds to enhance crunch and offer protein. Fruits are packed with vitamins, antioxidants, and fiber, and they make your breakfast vibrant and appealing! üåà 
3. **Veggie Omelette**: For something savory, whip up a veggie omelette filled with spinach, tomatoes, and mushrooms. Eggs are a powerhouse of protein and contain essential vitamins like B12. Add a side of whole-grain toast for some complex carbs and extra fiber.
4. **Smoothie Bowl**: Blend up a smoothie with your favorite greens, a banana, and a scoop of protein powder. Pour it into a bowl and top it with granola, seeds, and sliced fruits. This not only tastes delicious but looks like a work of art! When you "eat with your eyes," you feel more satisfied.
5. **Chia Pudding**: Combine chia seeds with your choice of milk and let it sit overnight to thicken. In the morning, top it with fruits, nuts, or a dollop of yogurt. Chia seeds are high in fiber and omega-3 fatty acids, making this dish a nutritional powerhouse!
