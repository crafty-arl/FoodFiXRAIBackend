### Grocery List for a Pescetarian Diet

1. **Seafood (Omega-3 Rich Foods)**:
   - **Wild Alaskan Salmon**: 
     - **Brand**: *Vital Choice* or *SeaBear*
     - **Health Benefits**: Rich in omega-3 fatty acids (EPA and DHA) supporting heart and brain health.
   
   - **Mackerel**: 
     - **Brand**: *King Oscar* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Loaded with omega-3s and vitamin D.
    
   - **Sardines**: 
     - **Brand**: *Wild Planet* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Convenient source of omega-3s, calcium, and selenium.

   - **Anchovies**: 
     - **Brand**: *Crown Prince* 
     - **Health Benefits**: High in omega-3s, supporting heart health.

2. **Plant-Based Proteins**:
   - **Tofu**: 
     - **Brand**: *Nasoya* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Excellent source of plant protein.
   
   - **Tempeh**: 
     - **Brand**: *Lightlife* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Fermented soy product providing probiotics.

   - **Lentils**: 
     - **Brand**: *Goya* (canned) 
     - **Health Benefits**: High in protein and fiber, low glycemic index.

   - **Chickpeas**: 
     - **Brand**: *Eden Foods* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Low glycemic option, rich in fiber.

3. **Probiotics for Gut Health**:
   - **Kefir**: 
     - **Brand**: *Lifeway* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Fermented drink packed with probiotics.
   
   - **Sauerkraut/Kimchi**: 
     - **Brand**: *Bubbies* or *Wildbrine* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Offers beneficial probiotics.

4. **Fruits and Vegetables**:
   - **Berries**: 
     - **Brand**: *Driscoll's Organic*
     - **Health Benefits**: Antioxidant-rich with a low glycemic index.

   - **Avocados**: 
     - **Health Benefits**: High in healthy fats and magnesium.
  
   - **Leafy Greens**: 
     - **Brand**: *Earthbound Farm Organic Spinach or Kale* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Packed with vitamins and minerals, including magnesium.

   - **Cruciferous Vegetables**: 
     - **Health Benefits**: Support gut health and provide fiber.

5. **Nuts and Seeds**:
   - **Chia Seeds**: 
     - **Brand**: *Nutiva* 
     - **Health Benefits**: High in omega-3s, fiber, and protein.

   - **Flaxseeds**: 
     - **Brand**: *Bob's Red Mill* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Great source of omega-3s; ground flaxseeds are easier to digest.

   - **Pumpkin Seeds**: 
     - **Brand**: *Terrasoul Superfoods* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Good source of magnesium and zinc.

6. **Low Glycemic Index Grains**:
   - **Quinoa**: 
     - **Brand**: *Ancient Harvest* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Complete protein and rich in magnesium.

   - **Brown Rice**: 
     - **Brand**: *Lundberg* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Low glycemic complex carbohydrate.

7. **Herbs and Spices**:
   - **Garlic**: 
     - **Health Benefits**: Contains compounds supporting heart health and nitric oxide production.

   - **Turmeric (Curcumin)**: 
     - **Brand**: *Simply Organic* 
     - **Health Benefits**: Anti-inflammatory properties and rich in antioxidants.

8. **Supplementary Nutrients**:
   - **Beets**: 
     - **Health Benefits**: Known for boosting nitric oxide levels, improving circulation.

   - **Spinach**: 
     - **Health Benefits**: Rich in nitrates that can support nitric oxide production.

9. **Selenium-Rich Foods**:
   - **Brazil Nuts**: 
     - **Brand**: *Terrasoul Superfoods* 
     - **Health Benefits**: One of the richest sources of selenium; a few are sufficient for daily requirements.

   - **Seafood Choices**: 
     - Cod and shrimp are naturally rich in selenium.

### Tips:
- Look for products labeled **sustainably sourced** and **wild-caught** to support ecological practices.
- Choose organic and non-GMO options for grains, nuts, and seeds to avoid pesticides and chemicals.
- Ensure variety in fruits and vegetables for diverse nutrient intake.

This grocery list will help you maintain a balanced pescetarian diet while focusing on omega-3s, gut health, magnesium, low glycemic index foods, and sustainable practices. Enjoy your nutritional journey!
